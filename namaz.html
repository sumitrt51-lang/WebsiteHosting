Information We Access or Collect

How We Use Location Information

The Application uses your location to provide core Islamic utility features, including:

  • Calculating accurate prayer times for your current city or coordinates.
  • Calculating Qibla direction from your current location.
  • Showing nearby mosques or map-based mosque results.
  • Improving location-based Islamic calendar and prayer-related features.

Your location may be processed by third-party services used by the Application, such as prayer time APIs, map services, geocoding services, Google Play Services, and advertising services, according to their own privacy policies.

We do not sell your precise location data. We do not use your precise location data for unrelated marketing purposes. You may disable location permission at any time from your device settings, but some features of the Application may not work properly without location access.
